Meet Stagedoor

IMDB for theatre.

Stagedoor is London’s most comprehensive and innovative theatre guide.

Stay up to date.

Follow your favourite directors, writers, actors and venues. Stay up-to-date with their current and upcoming shows.

Smart wishlist.

Keep track of shows you’re interested in. Get notified when tickets go on sale, reviews are out and before they close.

Instant book.

Seamlessly book tickets to shows. Say goodbye to entering CC details every time you book at a new venue.

Reviews. Reviews. Reviews.

Discover shows through thousands of audience and critic reviews. Keep track of all the shows you’ve seen in your theatre journal.

Important facts

Loved by Apple

With over 2 million apps, Apple’s app store is a crowded place. Imagine our surprise when Stagedoor got featured as one of the “Loved by Apple” apps. Twice! In one month!

Theatre festivals

Stagedoor is the official app of the biggest theatre festivals in London, The Camden Fringe 2018, 2017, and 2016, VAULT Festival 2017, listing over 1425 shows and 300 theatre companies.
