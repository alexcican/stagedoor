What we’re doing

We believe that theatre is for everyone, and by making it more accessible, we can bring more people close to it.

Stagedoor essentially levels the playing field between small and big budget productions. By giving a voice to the community, we believe that theatre goers can discover the best shows for them.

We are building a new way for theatre lovers to discover, book, and experience theatre. And we want you to be part of it.

Stagedoor funding.

For our second funding round we are raising £300,000 from Angel investors and strategic theatre industry partners. Our goal is to grow our user base to 100,000 in London, execute on our monetisation strategy, and prepare for geographical expansion in NYC. Our early stage investors and board members include tech entrepreneurs and theatre professionals. Being a very community-focused product, we wanted to open-up this funding round to our community and theatre lovers around the globe.
