What we’re doing

We believe that theatre is for everyone, and by making it more accessible, we can bring more people close to it.

Stagedoor essentially levels the playing field between small and big budget productions. By giving a voice to the community, we believe that theatre goers can discover the best shows for them.

We are building a new way for theatre lovers to discover, book, and experience theatre. And we want you to be part of it.
