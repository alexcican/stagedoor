What we’re doing

We are building a new way for theatre lovers to discover, book, and experience theatre.
And you can be part of it!

We believe that theatre is for everyone and that by making it more accessible, we can bring more people closer to it.

With Stagedoor, we want to level the playing field between small and big budget productions and believe that by giving a voice to the community, theatre goers can discover the best shows for them.
